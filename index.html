Welcome to Beat Machine

A place where I showcase my music and you control it. To get started, select a room. Each room is based on a music genre and is styled respectively. Click on an item in the room - Note: not all items are clickable - the item will begin playing a loop. the next one clicked won't play until the progress bar reaches the end. This is to ensure nothing gets played off beat! Play around with the audio on the right side, Enjoy!
